Cookie Policy

How we handle cookies and your preferences.

Last updated: May 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the content.

2. How We Use Cookies

RU Club Motherland uses cookies exclusively through Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymous traffic data. This helps us understand which pages are most popular, how visitors find us, and how we can improve the site experience.

We do NOT use cookies for:
  • Advertising or tracking across websites
  • Collecting personal identifiable information
  • Selling or sharing data with third parties

3. Data Collected

Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type and device type (anonymous)
  • Approximate geographic location (city-level only)
  • Referring website or traffic source

4. Your Consent

When you first visit our site, a cookie consent modal appears asking you to Accept or Decline analytics cookies. Your choice is stored in your browser's localStorage as cookie-consent.

  • Accepted: GA4 is granted analytics_storage permission
  • Declined: GA4 is denied analytics_storage — no tracking occurs

5. Managing Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, reach out to us at ruclubmotherland@gmail.com.
