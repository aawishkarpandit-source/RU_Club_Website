Cookie Policy

How we handle cookies and your privacy preferences.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They serve useful functions like remembering your preferences, understanding how you interact with content, and helping site owners improve their services.

2. Cookies We Use

RU Club Motherland uses cookies exclusively through Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — the only external cookie service on this site. GA4 helps us understand which pages are most visited, how users navigate the site, and what devices they use, so we can continuously improve the experience.

Cookie Details

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
_ga Distinguishes unique users 2 years Analytics
_ga_* Maintains session state 2 years Analytics

Local Storage (Not Cookies)

We also use browser localStorage (not cookies) for:

  • theme — Stores your light/dark mode preference (no expiry)
  • cookie-consent — Stores your Accept/Decline choice for the cookie banner

These values never leave your browser — they are not transmitted to any server.

We do NOT use cookies for:
  • Advertising, retargeting, or cross-site tracking
  • Collecting personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Selling or sharing data with third parties
  • Social media tracking or embedded widgets

3. Data Collected by GA4

When you accept cookies, Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system (anonymous user-agent data)
  • Approximate geographic location (city-level only, not precise GPS)
  • Referring website, search engine, or traffic source
  • Scroll depth, button clicks, and page interactions (events)

4. How Consent Works

On your first visit, a cookie consent banner appears at the bottom of the page with two options:

  • Accept All — GA4 is granted analytics_storage permission via the Consent Mode API. Cookies are set and tracking begins.
  • Decline — GA4 is denied analytics_storage. No cookies are set and no tracking occurs. The GA4 script loads but is configured to not send data.

Your choice is stored in localStorage as cookie-consent: "accepted" or cookie-consent: "declined". The banner will not reappear unless you clear your browser storage.

5. Managing & Revoking Consent

You can change your cookie preference at any time:

  • Clear localStorage in your browser's developer tools to reset consent and see the banner again
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete specific cookies via Settings → Privacy & Security

Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
