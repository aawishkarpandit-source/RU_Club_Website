Privacy Policy

How we handle your data with care and transparency.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Who We Are

RU Club Motherland is a student-led environmental sustainability club operating under Motherland Secondary School, Pokhara Metropolitan City - 7, Kaski, Gandaki Province, Nepal. This website is maintained by club members and faculty advisors as a public resource to share our missions, gallery, and contact information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve our website and serve you better:

  • Analytics data: Anonymous browsing patterns via Google Analytics GA4 (only loaded if you consent via the cookie banner)
  • Contact form data: Name, email address, and message you voluntarily submit through our contact form at /contact
  • Theme preference: Your light/dark mode choice is stored locally in your browser (not sent to any server)
  • Cookie consent preference: Your accept/decline choice is stored locally as cookie-consent

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries, questions, and partnership requests
  • Improve our website content, layout, and user experience
  • Understand aggregate visitor patterns (which pages are popular, how visitors find us)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for their own use. The only third-party services involved are:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — anonymous traffic analysis. Data is aggregated and anonymized. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. We send your name, email, and message to Formspree's servers. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts this website. Standard server logs (IP address, request timestamps) may be collected. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • When required by applicable law or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and are then deleted. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in accordance with Google's data retention policy. Server logs on Vercel are retained for 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics tracking at any time via the cookie banner
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns

7. Cookies

We use cookies exclusively through Google Analytics (GA4) for anonymous traffic measurement. A cookie consent banner on your first visit allows you to Accept or Decline. Your preference is stored in localStorage and can be changed at any time. See our full Cookie Policy for details.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (Facebook, Instagram, GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms. We encourage you to review their policies before engaging.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures including HTTPS with TLS encryption in transit, security headers (HSTS, X-Content-Type-Options, X-Frame-Options), and strict Permissions Policy. However, no online transmission is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

RU Club Motherland is a school-affiliated club, and some members may be under 18. However, our website does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to have it removed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be announced via our social media channels.
